Across rural Nepal, two silent crises play out inside school walls every day: contaminated drinking water and the absence of safe menstrual products that quietly push girls out of classrooms. The WASH Project addresses both at once — solar-powered water filtration and menstrual hygiene infrastructure, installed at 5 schools, built to last, and designed to replicate.
